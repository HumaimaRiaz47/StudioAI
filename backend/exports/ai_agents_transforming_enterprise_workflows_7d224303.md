### MoE-SLU in Medical Consultations
#### Overview
The early implementation of MoE-SLU (Mixture-of-Experts Speech-to-Text) in spoken medical consultations leveraged a mixture-of-experts model to improve keyword recognition accuracy. This approach has been shown to increase the reliability and efficiency of transcriptions.

**Technical Insight:**
MoE-SLU combines multiple speech recognition hypotheses, each with its own strengths, to produce more accurate and robust outputs. This results in improved keyword-recognition accuracy across various benchmarks.
